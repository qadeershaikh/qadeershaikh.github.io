Ocean Freight

The Ocean Freight division was started by RWLINDIA to break the barriers in logistics and make shipping via ocean simple for global business needs. The shipping of bigger volumes, ODC and hazardous cargo through the air is a challenge, hence ocean freight comes into the picture. We are experts in dealing with documentation and customs thus providing the best local solutions without any delay. Our real-time tracking of the shipment enables us to keep our clients always updated. Our strong networking across the globe has enabled us to create a world class supply chain eco system for our customers, understanding the complexity of your supply chain requirement, we have created a variety of services which are flexible to effectively integrate with your business. We work in close connection with all the major ocean lines to provide you reliable space allocation at best rates. We specialize in Project cargo handling, Vendor management, Freight procurement and offering complete 3PL/4PL solutions to our esteemed customer base. Feel free to get in touch with our Ocean Logistic Experts to get a quote today.

Our Services Include
  • Full container load (FCL), Less-than-container loads (LCL), Break-bulk services
  • Entire project consignments
  • Delivering port-to-port or end-to-end
  • Daily shipment status report
  • Import/export documentation audit
  • On and off-site transportation
