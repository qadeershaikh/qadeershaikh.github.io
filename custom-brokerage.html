Custom Brokerage

Local Custom Clearance is important for any trade. There are different guidelines set globally by all countries for export and import, moreover for all businesses dealing across borders is mandatory to abide by these rules to carry on trade. Getting all the clearances and documentation on time for your consignment can be very time-consuming and stressful. RWLINDIA with its global presence and experience in handling customs clearance is adept with all the documentation, giving a competitive edge to our clients. We aim at saving time along with all the efforts that are required to understand the technicalities and hence providing seamless access to the world trade for our clients. When it comes to import and export, every country and at times every port has different requirements. This requires a long list of papers and permits required to ensure that your cargo is safe and is not carrying any objectionable shipment.

Our Services Include
  • Procedure for custom clearance services
  • Documentation as required to complete the trade
  • Procedure related to export and import of goods and services 
  • Liaising with the concerned authorities for the submission of documents and permissions
